About

ADD International fights for independence, equality and opportunity for disabled people living in poverty in Africa and Asia.

Raising awareness

Disabled people are disproportionately vulnerable to all forms of abuse, face stigma and discrimination in their surroundings and get excluded from their communities, from the education system, from health care and other vital services.

Data Gathering

Our system gathers reports from disabled people and leaders of social groups in developing countries using various techniques to achieve valuable intelligence, which is then used to fill in gaps in our efforts to fight their discrimination.

Finding Solutions

Data is then used to fight misconceptions about disability by educating the society in the area and to provide equal opportunities for disabled, in terms of education, healthcare or support.
